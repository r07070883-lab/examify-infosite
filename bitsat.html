Purpose of the Exam

BITSAT is conducted by Birla Institute of Technology and Science (BITS) Pilani for admission into integrated first-degree programs offered at BITS Pilani, Goa, and Hyderabad campuses.

Important Dates

  • Registration: 15th December 2025 to 16th March 2026
  • Application correction: 18th-20th March 2026
  • Exam Dates: Session1 - 15th to 17th April , Session2 - 24th to 26th May
  • Result Declaration: Session1 - June 2026 , Session2 - July 2026
  • Counselling: July 2026

    • NOTE:

      Candidates can apply for Session-2 separately from 20th April to 2nd May 2026, if they missed the initial registration period or want to appear only in Session-2.

Registration

Official Registration Portal

Guide to Register

  1. Create an account on the BITS admission portal.
  2. Fill personal, academic, and contact details.
  3. Select preferred test centers.
  4. Upload photograph and signature.
  5. Pay the application fee online.
  6. Submit the form and download confirmation.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics.
  • Minimum 75% aggregate in PCM.
  • Minimum 60% marks in each subject.
  • Only one attempt per year.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Computer Based Test.
  • Total Questions: 130.
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English, Logical Reasoning.
  • Marking Scheme: +3 correct, -1 incorrect.

Syllabus

Based on NCERT Class 11 & 12 syllabus with additional logical reasoning and English.

Payment Details

  • Debit / Credit Card

  • UPI
  • Application fee is non-refundable

Documents Required

  • Passport-size photograph
  • Candidate signature
  • Class 10 & 12 mark sheets
  • Valid Photo ID
