NEET (UG) 2025

Purpose

The National Eligibility cum Entrance Test (NEET) is conducted by NTA for admission to undergraduate medical, dental, and AYUSH courses in India.

Courses Offered

  • MBBS ‚Äì Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery
  • BDS ‚Äì Bachelor of Dental Surgery
  • BAMS, BHMS, BUMS ‚Äì Traditional Medicine Courses
  • BSMS, BNYS ‚Äì Siddha and Yoga Courses

Important Dates

  • Application Start: February 2025 (tentative)
  • Exam Date: 3rd May 2025 (Offline Mode)
  • Result Declaration: June 2025

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English.
  • Minimum 50% marks (40% for OBC/SC/ST).
  • Minimum age: 17 years by 31st December 2025.

Exam Pattern

The exam has 200 questions (180 to be answered). Each subject ‚Äì Physics, Chemistry, Biology (Botany & Zoology) ‚Äì is divided into Section A (35 Qs) and Section B (15 Qs).

Negative marking: 1 mark deducted for each wrong answer. +4 for correct.

Exam Mode & Languages

NEET is conducted in **offline (pen-paper)** mode and available in **13 languages** including English, Hindi, Kannada, Tamil, Telugu, and more.

Participating Institutes

  • All AIIMS Institutes (Delhi, Bhopal, Bhubaneswar, etc.)
  • JIPMER Puducherry
  • Government & Private Medical Colleges under MCC/State Quotas

Documents Required

  • Class 10 & 12 Marksheets
  • Passport-size Photograph & Signature
  • Photo ID Proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
  • Category & PwD Certificate (if applicable)

Payment Methods

Payment can be made through Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI.

Syllabus

Based on NCERT Class 11 & 12 syllabus of Physics, Chemistry, and Biology.
