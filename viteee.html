VITEEE - VIT Entrance Exam

About VITEEE

VITEEE (Vellore Institute of Technology Engineering Entrance Examination) is a national-level entrance test conducted by VIT University for admission into B.Tech programs at its campuses in Vellore, Chennai, Bhopal, and Amaravati.

Important Dates

  • Application Start: November 2024
  • Last Date to Apply: March 2025
  • Exam Dates: April 2025
  • Result Declaration: May 2025

Eligibility Criteria

  • Candidate must have passed or appeared in Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology.
  • Minimum aggregate: 60% for general candidates, 50% for reserved categories.
  • Age Limit: Born on or after July 1, 2003.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online (Computer-Based Test)
  • Duration: 2 hours 30 minutes
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology, English, Aptitude
  • Total Marks: 125 (no negative marking)

Documents Required for Registration

  • Recent passport-size photograph and signature
  • Class 10 and 12 marksheets
  • Valid ID proof (Aadhar/PAN/Passport)
  • Community/Category certificate (if applicable)

Payment Methods

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, Rupay)
  • UPI or Net Banking
  • Demand Draft (for offline mode)

Interesting Facts about VIT

  • VIT has consistently been ranked among the top private engineering colleges in India.
  • It holds a record for over 10,000 placement offers in a single season.
  • VIT introduced a unique “Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS)” allowing students to choose their own subjects and timetable.
  • Campuses have international collaborations with 400+ foreign universities.
  • Known for its modern infrastructure, vibrant campus life, and student diversity from across India and 50+ countries.
