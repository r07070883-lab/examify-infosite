WBJEE - West Bengal Joint Entrance Exam

About WBJEE

The West Bengal Joint Entrance Examination (WBJEE) is a state-level exam conducted by the WBJEE Board for admission to engineering, architecture, and pharmacy programs in colleges across West Bengal.

Important Dates

  • Application Start: December 2024
  • Last Date to Apply: January 2025
  • Exam Date: April 2025
  • Results Declaration: May 2025

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Minimum marks: 45% (General), 40% (Reserved).
  • Minimum age: 17 years as of December 31, 2025.
  • Indian nationality required.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Offline (OMR-based)
  • Papers: Paper-I (Mathematics), Paper-II (Physics & Chemistry)
  • Duration: 2 hours per paper
  • Negative Marking: -0.25 for Category-I questions

Documents Required for Registration

  • Recent photograph and signature
  • 10th and 12th mark sheets
  • Domicile certificate (for state quota)
  • Caste/category certificate (if applicable)

Payment Methods

  • Online payment via Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI
  • No offline mode accepted
