CUET (UG) — Exam Information

Purpose

CUET (UG) provides a common entrance test for admission to various Central and participating universities across India.

Important Dates

  • Registration expected around March
  • Exam usually conducted in May–June
  • Admit card a few days before exam
Always refer to the official CUET NTA website for the latest and exact schedule.
CUET Official Syllabus Guide to Register

Documents Required

  • Passport size photograph
  • Signature
  • Class 10 & 12 documents
  • Photo ID

Exam Pattern

Multiple choice CBT exam with language papers, domain subjects and general test.
