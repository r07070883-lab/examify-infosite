Guide to Register for CUET (UG)

Step-by-Step Registration Process

Step 1 — Visit Official Website

Go to cuet.nta.nic.in and click on “Registration for CUET (UG)”.

Step 2 — Create a New Account
  • Enter your name, DOB, gender, mobile no., and email.
  • Choose a strong password.
  • Verify OTP.
Step 3 — Fill Application Details
  • Personal and address details
  • Education details (Class 10 & 12)
  • Select your preferred universities & programs
  • Select required subject papers
Step 4 — Upload Documents
  • Passport-size photo
  • Signature
  • Category/PwD certificate (if applicable)
Step 5 — Pay Fees

Pay using Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, UPI.

Step 6 — Download Confirmation Page

Save and print your confirmation page for future reference.
