Purpose of the Exam

JEE Main is conducted by the National Testing Agency (NTA) for admission into B.E./B.Tech, B.Arch and B.Planning programs in NITs, IIITs, and other centrally funded institutions. It is also the qualifying exam for JEE Advanced.

Important Dates

  • Session 1 Exam: January 2025
  • Session 2 Exam: April 2025
  • Registration Start: November 2024
  • Admit Card Release: 7 days before exam

Registration

Official Registration Portal

Step-by-Step Guide to Register

  1. Visit the official JEE Main website.
  2. Click on "New Registration" and create a candidate profile.
  3. Fill personal, academic, and contact details carefully.
  4. Upload photograph, signature, and required certificates.
  5. Select exam city preferences.
  6. Pay the application fee online.
  7. Download confirmation page for future reference.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Class 12 in 2023, 2024, or appearing in 2025.
  • Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics mandatory subjects.
  • No age limit prescribed by NTA.
  • Maximum 3 attempts in consecutive years.

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Computer Based Test (CBT)
  • Duration: 3 Hours
  • Total Questions: 90 (75 to be attempted)
  • Marking: +4 correct, -1 incorrect
  • Sections: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
