MET – Manipal Entrance Test

Purpose of the Exam

Manipal Entrance Test (MET) is conducted by Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) for admission into undergraduate engineering programs offered at Manipal and other MAHE campuses.

Important Dates

  • Application Start: October
  • Last Date to Apply: March
  • Exam Dates: April – May
  • Counselling: June

Registration

Step-by-step Guide:

  • Visit the MAHE admissions portal
  • Create an account using email & mobile number
  • Fill personal and academic details
  • Upload photograph and signature
  • Pay the application fee
  • Submit and download the application form
Official Registration Link

Eligibility Criteria

  • Passed Class 12 with Physics, Mathematics, and one optional subject
  • Minimum aggregate marks as specified by MAHE
  • No age limit specified

Exam Pattern

  • Mode: Online (CBT)
  • Duration: 2 hours
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English
  • MCQ-based questions

Syllabus

Based on Class 11 and 12 syllabus of Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

Download Syllabus PDF

Payment Details

  • Application Fee: ₹2000 (approx)
  • Payment via Credit/Debit Card, Net Banking, or UPI
  • Fee is non-refundable

Documents Required

  • Recent passport-size photograph
  • Signature
  • Class 10 & 12 marksheets
  • Government photo ID
