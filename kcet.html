KCET 2025

Purpose

The Karnataka Common Entrance Test (KCET) is conducted by KEA for admission to engineering, pharmacy, and agriculture courses in Karnataka colleges.

Important Dates

  • Application Start: March 2025 (tentative)
  • Exam Date: May 2025
  • Result Declaration: June 2025

Eligibility Criteria

  • Must have passed Class 12 with PCM/PCB.
  • Should be a domicile of Karnataka (or satisfy eligibility norms).

Exam Pattern

KCET consists of 3 papers — Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics/Biology. Each paper carries 60 marks, with no negative marking.

Documents Required

  • SSLC & PUC Marksheets
  • Study Certificate / Domicile Proof
  • Photo ID & Recent Photograph

Payment Methods

Payment can be made through Debit/Credit Card, Net Banking, or at designated banks via challan.

Syllabus

Based on the Department of Pre-University Education (PUC) syllabus of Karnataka for Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics/Biology.
